Health Tips

#1. Make sure your working area, utensils and hands have been cleaned
with warm soapy water before begining to cook.

#2. Before cooking, tie back any long hair, and ensure any long clothing is out the way or removed.

#3. Keep separate the measuring, mixing and handling of unbake batter or dough from cooling,
serving of baked goods.

#4. Test baked food with a wooden toothpick or cake tester and food thermometer
at the center to ensure prodcuts are completly baked.

#5. Wash your hands again before you taste, serve or store your baked food.
