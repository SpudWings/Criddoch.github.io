Scone Recipes

Fruit Scone

Fruit scones are a classic and easy to make scone recipe that is perfect for an afternoon tea. Can be served with jam or clotted cream.

Gluten Free Scone

Gluten free scones are a great alternative to the traditional scone. They are made with gluten free flour and are a great option for those who
are gluten intolerant or have coeliac disease. They are also a great option for those who are trying to cut down
on gluten in their diet.

Classic Cheese Scone

These light and fluffy cheese scones are easy to make and topped with a toasted cheese crust.
They make a delicious savoury treat for afternoon tea.
