Lardy Cake

Preparation: 15 mins   Difficulty: Easy
Serves: 8-10   Cook Time: 30 mins

Ingredients                     Method

225g/8 oz flour

¼ teaspoon salt

¼ teaspoon mixed spice

¼ oz fresh yeast

150ml/ ¼ pint lukewarm milk

50g / 2 oz lard

50g/ 2 oz sugar

50g/ 2 oz mixed dried fruit

sugar and water glaze

STEP 1
Sift the flour, salt and spice into a bowl and warm in the oven.
Cream the yeast with a little of the milk and leave in a warm place until frothy.

STEP 2
Add the flour mixture with enough of the remaining milk to make a soft dough.
Beat well, then cover and leave ina warm place until doubled in size.

STEP 3
Roll out the dough on a well-floured board to an until its an ablong 5mm/ ¼ inch in size.
Spread on half the lard and sprinkle with half the sugar and half the fruit.
STEP 4
Fold in three, turn to the left as for flaky pastry and roll out again.
Cover with the remaining lard, sugar and fruit. Fold in three again, turn and roll out to an oblong 2.5cm/ 1 inch thick peice.
STEP 5
Place in a deep baking tin and leave in a warm place until well risen.
Score the top of the cake with a knife and brush with sugar and water glaze.
Bake in a hot oven (200°C/400°F, Gas Mark 6) for 30 minutes.
