Banana Cake

Preparation: 15 mins   Difficulty: Easy
Serves: 8-10   Cook Time: 30 mins

Ingredients                     Method

225g softened butter

2-3 very ripe bananas

1 teaspoon lemon juice

100g/4 OZ butter or margarine

100g/4 OZ caster sugar

2 eggs, beaten

225g/8 OZ self-raising flour

lemon icing (optional)

STEP 1
Mash the banans with the lemon juice. Cream together the fat and sugar, then beat
the eggs followed by the banana. Sift the flour and mix into banana mixture.

STEP 2
Turn into a greased 15x20 cm/6x8 inch cake tin (or prefered size) and bake
in a moderately hot oven (200°C/400°F, Gass Mark 6) for 40 to 45 minutes.

STEP 3
Cool on a wire rack. If you like, ice the cake with a lemon icing, but it is very nice without.
